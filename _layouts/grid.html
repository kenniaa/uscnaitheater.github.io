Dickens Day & Night

USC NAI Dickens Day & Night 2016 was a campus-wide thematic learning experience based on the works and times of Charles Dickens. With the Dickens Project, the USC NAI Theater Workshop and Exploring the Arts, South LA seniors produced arts projects bringing their study of the novel, the annual Dickens Universe selection, to life.
